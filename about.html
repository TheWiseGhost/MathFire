About This Website
Math Fire is a trainer designed to help high school math team students suceed during their competitions. Using advanced problems and a train by problem design, Math Fire helps a student work on their weaknesses and achieve mastery. While other trainers may provide irrelvant information or problems outside the scope of high school competetions, Math Fire is built by a high school student for high school students, packing value and insight into every problem.
